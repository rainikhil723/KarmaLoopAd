How It Actually Works

We eliminated the "confusing and complicated" barriers identified in our survey. It takes exactly three steps to build an unbreakable habit.

1. The Mobile App: Action

+

Open the KarmaLoop app when you start studying. Select your subject, set your target time, and enter the distraction-free Focus Mode. Every minute logged is securely synced to our servers.

2. The Point Algorithm: Reward

+

Our algorithm converts your session into Karma Points. You earn base points for time, multipliers for maintaining a daily streak, and bonus points for hitting specific academic milestones.

3. The Web Dashboard: Analysis

+

Log into the web platform to view the big picture. See your heatmap of active days, analyze which subjects drain the most time, and check your global ranking on the student leaderboard.
